'.' - matches every thing escept new line.
\d - digits(0-9)
\w - word character.(a-z  A-Z 0-9)
\W - not a word character.
\s - whitespaces(space,tab,newline)
\S - not whitespaces
\b - word boundery
\B - Not a word Boundary
^  - Begening of a string
$  - End of a string
[] - matches characters in Brackets
[^ ] - matches characters not in Brackets

|  - Either or
() - Group

Quantifires:
* - Zero or more
+ - one or more
? - Zero or one
{3}-Exact number
{1,4}-Range of the number(minimum-maximum)
